About Kubrick Con

Kubrick Con will be held at the historic Hollywood Theatre in beautiful Portland, Oregon!

Explore the genius of Stanley Kubrick through his films and expert presentations on subjects surrounding his carrer.

Kubrick Con is committed to being inclusive and enjoyable for everyone from the most hardcore fans to those who are experiencing the work of Stanley Kubrick for the first time. We look forward to another intensive weekend of world class cinema and sharing.

New This Year

Kubrick Con 2017 has expanded its program to two full days and now includes two additional films as well as 70mm projection for selected screenings.

In addition, we have added a third guest presentation slot and an after party where attendants can meet our guest speakers and interact with them one on one, as well as several Kubrick themed raffle prizes and a gallery of original props and memorabilia associated with the films.
